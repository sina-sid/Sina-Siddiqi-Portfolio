Fenrir

A retelling of the Three Little Pigs story using Norse mythology

This epic game is based on FENRIR, the monstrous wolf from Norse mythology. His son Skoll has been kidnapped by three pigs, namely the straw, wood and brick pigs. Seeking revenge the player takes on the role of Fenrir to rescue his son by killing the three big pigs.

The feel of this game reminds one of a pop up book. There are three levels in this game in which the player uses the arrow keys to move the wolf around and attack the enemy pigs.

My role was of a UX designer and I made all cut scenes and UI elements.

This game was developed in five weeks by Alight Studios which comprised of a team of 5 CMU students. This game is a reflection of all the things we learnt and all the assets used in the game were created by the team.
