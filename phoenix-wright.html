Phoenix Wright Text Adventure

A text adventure based on the popular Phoenix Wright Trilogy

A notorious yet arrogant college student was murdered in his dorm room and his roommate, Kip Treblemike, is the primary suspect! The cause of death? Ingesting poisonous mustard with his... Chinese food. Kip has asked you, Phoenix Wright, the talented and famous defense attorney, to help him prove his innocence. Problem is, there's a lot of evidence against him, including his odd relationship with the victim.

This was a solo project completed in under a week. I spent about 40+ hours writing the story and putting it together via code. Though it's just a text adventure, it is by far, the most complicated storyline I have ever written and I had a great time both writing and putting it together.
