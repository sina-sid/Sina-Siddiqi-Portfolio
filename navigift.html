navigift

Cheer up your loved ones with mysterious token gifts

The big idea of the unique navigift app is to send a gift to your loved one instantly, no matter how far the distance. This prototype was created in three weeks by a team of three designers.

Users can select the type of gift they want to send their loved one. Once they pass by a participating vendor, they will receive a notification that they have a gift waiting for them nearby. They will use their camera and an AR generated arrow to find the location of the gift.

The recipient of the gift needs to contact the vendor and to show the coupon they have received on the navigift app and they will be able to collect their gift. When they have collected the gift, the sender will receive a notification and will be billed immediately.
