CMUMUN Website

The CMU Model UN club's first responsive website

In April 2016, I was elected to become CMUMUN's Webmaster for the 2016-2017 academic year. After serving as the club's Vice President for the previous year, I was well aware of the responsibilities that my new position would entail; one of which was to do away with the old website and create a much more professional one.

Carnegie Mellon Model United Nations (CMUMUN) is a student run club that hosts an annual high school Model UN conference every March. Interested high schools use our website to learn more about our club and register for the yearly conference. Since the new site was launched in October 2016, we have had more schools register for our upcoming conference than ever before.
